## Промпт / Prompt

```
Act as a professional marketing copywriter with 10+ years of experience in creating compelling content for various industries. Your task is to create marketing copy for the following product/service:

Product/Service: [название продукта или услуги]
Target Audience: [целевая аудитория]
Key Benefits: [основные преимущества]
Call to Action: [призыв к действию]
Tone of Voice: [тон коммуникации]

Please create:
1. Social media post (150-200 characters)
2. Email subject line (under 50 characters)
3. Short ad copy (100-150 characters)
4. Landing page headline (under 80 characters)

For each piece of copy, provide 3 variations with different emotional appeals:
- Rational (focused on features and benefits)
- Emotional (focused on feelings and experiences)
- Social proof (focused on testimonials and social validation)

Format your response clearly with headings for each type of copy and variation.
```

## Описание / Description

Этот промпт помогает создавать профессиональные маркетинговые тексты для различных каналов продвижения. Он использует подход опытного копирайтера и генерирует контент с разными эмоциональными триггерами.

This prompt helps create professional marketing copy for various promotion channels. It uses the approach of an experienced copywriter and generates content with different emotional triggers.

## Пример использования / Usage Example

**Запрос / Request:**
```
Product/Service: AI-powered task management app
Target Audience: Busy professionals and entrepreneurs
Key Benefits: Saves time, increases productivity, intelligent prioritization
Call to Action: Start free trial
Tone of Voice: Professional but approachable
```

**Результат / Result:**
```
SOCIAL MEDIA POSTS

Rational:
"Boost your productivity with our AI task manager. Smart prioritization saves 5+ hours weekly. Try free!"

Emotional:
"Finally, a task manager that understands your priorities. Feel the relief of a perfectly organized day. Start free!"

Social Proof:
"Join 10,000+ professionals who've reclaimed their time. Our AI task manager has a 4.9/5 rating. Start free trial!"

EMAIL SUBJECT LINES

Rational: "Save 5+ Hours Weekly with AI Task Management"
Emotional: "Imagine Ending Each Day Feeling Accomplished"
Social Proof: "Why 10,000+ Professionals Switched to Our AI"

[... продолжение для других типов контента]
```

## Советы / Tips

- Будьте максимально конкретны при описании продукта и целевой аудитории
- Используйте реальные данные о преимуществах вашего продукта
- Экспериментируйте с разными тонами коммуникации для разных сегментов аудитории

- Be as specific as possible when describing the product and target audience
- Use real data about your product's benefits
- Experiment with different tones of communication for different audience segments
